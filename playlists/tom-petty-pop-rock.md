# Tom Petty Pop/Rock

A pop/rock playlist headlining Tom Petty — ten of his essential tracks (with The Heartbreakers, solo, and a duet with Stevie Nicks) interwoven with peer pop/rock hits from the late '70s through the mid-'90s. Heartland melodies, jangly guitars, and radio-ready hooks from the era Petty defined.

## Playlist Instructions

### Management

- The playlist is defined in this file — this is the source of truth
- Use the **playlist-creator** skill for rebuilding or updating
- Use the **apple-music-api** skill for syncing to Apple Music

### Ordering

- Loosely chronological by release year, with Tom Petty tracks distributed throughout so he headlines the playlist from start to finish
- Adjacent tracks are sequenced for flow — do not shuffle

### Scope

- Pop/rock and heartland rock from ~1976 through ~1996
- Tom Petty (with The Heartbreakers and solo) is the headlining artist; he should remain the most-represented act on the playlist
- Studio recordings only — see `PREFERENCES.md` for the studio-version policy
