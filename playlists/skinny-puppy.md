# Skinny Puppy

Complete studio discography for Skinny Puppy, organized by album in chronological order.

## Playlist Instructions

### Management

- The playlist is defined in this file — this is the source of truth
- Use the **artist-catalog** skill for rebuilding or updating
- Use the **apple-music-sync** skill for syncing to Apple Music
- Sync with: `node .github/skills/apple-music-sync/sync.mjs playlists/skinny-puppy.md --headless`

### Ordering

- Tracks are ordered by album release date, then by track number within each album
- Do not shuffle — the chronological album order is intentional

### Notes

- **HanDover** (2011) is not available on US Apple Music and is omitted from this playlist
- Apple Music lists the album title as "VIVI Sect VI" (the original stylization is "VIVIsectVI")
- "The Greater Wrong of the Right" uses the Remastered edition on Apple Music
- "Mythmaker" uses the Deluxe edition on Apple Music; bonus remixes are excluded
- "Spahn Dirge (Live)" bonus track on Rabies is excluded per studio-over-live preference
